Motivation & Mindset

I’ve struggled with motivation. In school, I chased high marks with minimal effort. That mindset followed me into university, where I hit some lows—but also realized I needed to change. I found Dr. K, a psychiatrist YouTuber, and his video on "Puer Aeternus" (men of inaction) really resonated with me. It showed me that effort isn’t something to be avoided—it's the path to growth.

I now aspire to learn properly. I’ve started PyTorch, slowly escaping tutorial hell. I’m also building this site in raw HTML & CSS, plus a little JavaScript, my second personal webiste! It may look amateur, but I’m proud of it. I’m creating for me, and if you like it too, even better!

Thanks for reading!
